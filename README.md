## The Four Hubs (What They Actually Do)

### 1. Patient Hub ‚Äî Your Complete Health Identity

**Authentication That Makes Sense**
- Log in once with Patient ID + phone number
- No duplicate accounts, no data scattered across platforms

**Your Medical Timeline**
- Every diagnosis, treatment, prescription in one place
- Auto-updates after every doctor visit
- Visual health trends from your wearables

**Book Appointments Like a Human**
- GPS finds nearby hospitals
- Shows actual wait times and token numbers
- Automatic reminders so you don't forget

**SymptoSphere ‚Äî AI Symptom Checker (Not a Doctor)**
- Describe what's wrong
- Get initial guidance on home care or when to seek help
- It's decision support, not diagnosis‚Äîbig difference

**Talk to Your Doctor**
- Text or video consultations
- AI assistant trained on how your doctor communicates
- Works in multiple languages because not everyone speaks English

**Emergency? One Tap**
- Books nearest ambulance instantly
- Route optimized for traffic, not just distance
- Your medical history travels with the ambulance crew

**Lab Results & Imaging**
- Upload X-rays, blood reports, scans
- AI helps spot patterns and suggests next steps
- Treatment roadmaps based on actual medical protocols

**Digital Prescriptions**
- Linked to your Aadhaar
- Complete history of what you've been prescribed
- Compare government vs private pharmacy prices

**Insurance Without the Headache**
- Track policies and renewals
- Automatic alerts before expiry
- Hospital billing integration

**Smart Device Integration**
- Wearables feed real-time vitals
- Early warning system for concerning trends
- Auto-notifies your doctor and emergency services if needed

**Hologram Trainer ‚Äî Emergency Response Coach**
- Phone camera shows AR guide for CPR, first aid
- Step-by-step visual instructions when every second counts

**Government Schemes Access**
- Connect to UMANG and welfare programs
- AI helps you fill applications correctly

**Emergency Survival Guide**
- Location-aware crisis assistance
- Example: Snake bite? Shows nearest antivenom source and what to do now

**Medical Crowdfunding**
- Raise funds for treatment with verified documentation
- Direct support channel
